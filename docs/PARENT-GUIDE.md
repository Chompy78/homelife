# Getting started (for parents)

## 1. Join
You'll be sent a **parent code** (e.g. `UVGF-MA7X`). Go to the parent dashboard
and enter it once - your device remembers it after that.
`https://<your-username>.github.io/homelife/apps/parent-dashboard/`

## 2. Set up your kids
In the dashboard:
- **Add a kid** - enter their name and pick an avatar. You'll get back a **kid
  code** (e.g. `AA6-B7E`) and a "Copy link" button.
- Check the **family PIN** shown in Settings (default `2468`) - this is what
  you'll enter on their tablet to confirm a room pass. Change it if you like.

## 3. Install on each kid's tablet
1. Open Chrome or Edge on their tablet.
2. Go to `.../apps/bedroom-reset/` (or paste the "Copy link" URL, which fills
   their code in automatically).
3. Enter their kid code once.
4. Tap the browser menu → **Install app** / **Add to Home screen**.
5. Open it from the home screen from now on - it remembers them.

## Day to day
- They tick off jobs; when the room's done, they tap **Pass** or **Great
  Job** and you enter the family PIN to confirm it.
- You can see everyone's progress any time from the parent dashboard.
- If you've added a shared room (like the kitchen), it shows up as a second
  tab at the top of their app - any kid can open and help finish it.

## Optional
- **Public leaderboard**: turn on "Share our stats" in Settings to appear on
  `.../apps/leaderboard/` alongside other families (totals only, never your
  kids' names or rooms).
